How Pricing Works

Prices = Probabilities

YES @ $0.65 means the market thinks there's a 65% chance of YES. Buying YES at $0.65 pays $1.00 if correct — profit of $0.35 per share.

Arbitrage Signal

YES + NO should always sum to ~$1.00. If YES=$0.60 and NO=$0.38, they sum to $0.98. Buy both sides for $0.98, collect $1.00 at resolution = guaranteed 2% profit regardless of outcome.

ROI Calculation

Buying YES at $0.30 means you think probability is higher than 30%. If correct: $1.00 payout / $0.30 cost = 3.33× return. Each cent of edge = profit.

CLOB vs AMM

Polymarket uses a Central Limit Order Book. Prices shown are the last traded price. The order book shows pending bids/asks — you can set limit orders at any price.

📚 How These Strategies Work

RSI (Relative Strength Index)

Measures how overbought or oversold an asset is. RSI > 60 = upward momentum. RSI < 40 = downward momentum. RSI 40–60 = neutral, no trade.

Momentum

Price now vs. price 5 candles ago. If BTC is up 0.3%+ in 5 minutes, momentum is strongly bullish. Negative momentum = bearish. Fast and direct.

MACD

12-period EMA minus 26-period EMA vs. its 9-period signal line. When MACD crosses above signal line = buy trigger. Cross below = sell trigger.

Volume Spike

Current candle volume vs. 20-candle average. A spike (>1.5×) with positive price = institutional buying. Confirms direction signals.

Market Edge

Polymarket's 5-min BTC market usually prices near 50/50. When your indicators say 65%+ confidence UP but market says 50%, that's edge — buy UP at 50¢ for a fair value of 65¢.

Composite Score

Weighted combination: RSI 30% + Momentum 35% + MACD 25% + Volume 10%. Score > 60 = BUY UP signal. Score < 40 = BUY DOWN signal. 40–60 = no trade.
