How Pricing Works

Prices = Probabilities

YES @ $0.65 means the market thinks there's a 65% chance of YES. Buying YES at $0.65 pays $1.00 if correct — profit of $0.35 per share.

Arbitrage Signal

YES + NO should always sum to ~$1.00. If YES=$0.60 and NO=$0.38, they sum to $0.98. Buy both sides for $0.98, collect $1.00 at resolution = guaranteed 2% profit regardless of outcome.

ROI Calculation

Buying YES at $0.30 means you think probability is higher than 30%. If correct: $1.00 payout / $0.30 cost = 3.33× return. Each cent of edge = profit.

CLOB vs AMM

Polymarket uses a Central Limit Order Book. Prices shown are the last traded price. The order book shows pending bids/asks — you can set limit orders at any price.
